Getting Started

Team Titanic has created this site in the hopes of helping immigrants find meaningful employment in a comparable profession. Often immigrants already have all of the skills, they lack formal accredidation and/or cultural/language training. We help connect funding to these individuals so they can pursue these education opportunities in the short-term. Whether your supporting or raising money, start by building your profile today!
