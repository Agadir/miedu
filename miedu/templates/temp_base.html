How to contribute

So much to do and so few hands to help. MIEDU is an open-source project spearheaded by MIDROID Inc. to make education more accessible. Check us out on Github, send us a pull request. We're also looking for help on internationalization making our materials available for everyone around the globe and editors/curators of our learning materials section. Do you have a special area of expertise? Join us in creating an authoritative platform for education. Find out more
